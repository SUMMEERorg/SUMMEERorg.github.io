The Sustainable Material Management Extreme Events Reconnaissance (SUMMEER) organization aims to facilitate data collection, develop reconnaissance guidance, train stakeholders, share resources, and support innovative research. Through these activities, their ultimate mission is to enable and advance the sustainable management of post-disaster materials.
