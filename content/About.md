**Abstract-** As a Graduate Research Assistant at RIDER Center, I conduct multi-disciplinary research on various subjects related to sustainable built environment, automation in construction, data-driven decision-making, and transportation systems analysis. I am also a Ph.D. candidate in Civil Engineering at Florida State University, where I apply my knowledge and skills in Intelligent Transportation Systems, AI, and large-scale decision-making to address complex and dynamic problems in the context of transportation systems.
My research focuses on sustainable infrastructure systems, intelligent transportation systems, reinforcement learning applications, and transportation planning under uncertainty.
