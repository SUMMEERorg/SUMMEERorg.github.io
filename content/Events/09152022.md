# Disaster Reconnaissance for Sustainable Post-Disaster Materials Management

- September 15-16, 2022
- SUMMEER Second Workshop Recording


SUMMEER organized its second interdisciplinary workshop, bringing together a diverse group of participants ranging from engineering to social sciences and arts from both academic and industry communities. The two-day in-person workshop took place in San Luis Obispo, California, on the 15th and 16th of September 2022. The workshop included general presentations and targeted breakout sessions that aimed to (i) identify tools and techniques for reconnaissance activities, (ii) identify challenges in field reconnaissance, and (iii) identify and develop preliminary reconnaissance guidelines and standards.
